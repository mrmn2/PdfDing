# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

The PdfDing team and community take security bugs seriously. We appreciate your efforts to
responsibly disclose your findings, and will make every effort to acknowledge your contributions.

To report a security issue, please use the GitHub Security Advisory
[Report a Vulnerability](https://github.com/mrmn2/PdfDing/security/advisories/new) tab.

The team will send a response indicating the next steps in handling your report as quickly
as possible.
